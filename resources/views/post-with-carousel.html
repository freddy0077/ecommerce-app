Bicycle NXT 3000 exhibited at CES in Vegas

Posted by Shane Doe
Dec 15, 201625

NXT 3000 Electric Bikes will be soon available as the world's most powerful hybrid electric bikes. These model will be powered with enhanced pedaling system, as well as a speed accelerator - a 100 horsepower engine, with maximum speed at 100km/h. NXT 3000 Electric Bikes are lightweight, fast, tough and entertaining to ride.

The latest technological and startup fair, held annually in Las Vegas, NV - CES 2015, has seen our electronic bikes startup achieving popular acclaim and accolades. Such an enthusiasm inspires us to finish up our overall work on the bike as soon as possible. Tom Brown, the founder of a well known vehicle manufacturer "Tesla", was very interested in our project.
